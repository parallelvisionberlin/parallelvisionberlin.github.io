Parallel Vision / Material Research

Material Research for Bodies in Transition

Fashion After Fabric explores what clothing becomes when textiles are replaced by responsive, biological and synthetic systems.

Each collection develops a distinct material hypothesis—from liquid metal and programmable surfaces to engineered tissue, magnetic structures and living membranes.

Together, they form an evolving archive of possible relationships between clothing, technology and the human body.
