FLESH ZERO / 02

Flesh Zero is the point where the body stops being dressed and begins being replaced. Chrome, gas, foam and volatile synthetic tissue form a ceremonial surface around the human silhouette. The collection belongs to the Parallel Vision Berlin 2063 timeline and expands the Liquid Metal language into something more organic, ritualistic and unstable than Chromia.
