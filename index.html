Parallel Vision

Independent label from Berlin

About

Parallel Vision is a Berlin-based imprint for raw, emotional electronic music. The catalog moves where new wave, electro, and indie dance collide — dark yet luminous, minimal yet powerful. Founded by Alejandro Molinari in 2025, the label curates forward-thinking works that feel ritualistic and cinematic on the floor.
