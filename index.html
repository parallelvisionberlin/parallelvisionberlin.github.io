Berlin 2063

Parallel Vision is not only a label. It is a future city, a sound archive and a visual world.

In 2063, artificial intelligence predicts reality before it happens. Synthetic guardians patrol the city. Human memory is stored in the cloud. The night still belongs to the few signals that cannot be calculated.

Parallel Vision documents those signals through music, images and fragments from a future Berlin that feels ancient, romantic and dangerous.

Music remains
the last unexplained
anomaly.
