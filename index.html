Berlin 2063

Parallel Vision documents Berlin 2063: a city of managed systems, artificial memory, nightlife residues and emotional signals that prediction cannot fully contain.

In this version of Berlin, infrastructure still runs after collapse. Trains move, towers breathe, archives remember, and desire survives inside machine order.

Parallel Vision follows the unstable frequencies left between bodies, music, architecture and memory. Not prophecy. Not nostalgia. A living fragment from a future already rehearsing itself.

Music remains the last unstable signal.
