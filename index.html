Parallel Vision

Independent label from Berlin

About

Parallel Vision is an independent Berlin-based label exploring the edges of indie dance, new wave, and electro. Founded by Alejandro Molinari in 2025, it serves as a platform for forward-thinking artists redefining the underground sound.
